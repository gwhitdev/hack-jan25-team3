Valuable Resources on Human Trafficking in Western Europe

If you are concerned about human trafficking or want to take action, many organisations and resources in Western Europe can provide support, information, and assistance. Here is a list of key contacts and services:

Organizations and Hotlines

  1. European Union Anti-Trafficking Coordination
    • Website: ec.europa.eu/anti-trafficking
    • Provides policy guidance, reports, and an overview of anti-trafficking efforts across Europe.
  2. La Strada International
    • Website: lastradainternational.org
    • A network of European NGOs working to combat trafficking and provide victim support.
  3. Anti-Slavery International
    • Website: antislavery.org
    • Works to eliminate modern slavery and human trafficking globally.
  4. A21 Campaign
    • Website: a21.org
    • Has a European presence with resources and advocacy efforts.
    • Hotline: Varies by country; see website for local contacts.

    5. National Hotlines

    • Ireland: Blue Blindfold Campaign –
      +353 1800 666 111
    • United Kingdom: Modern Slavery Helpline –
      +44 08000 121 700
    • France: National Human Trafficking Hotline –
      +33 0800 200 777
    • Germany: Advice Centre for Human Trafficking –
      +49 30 2639 6736
    • Spain: National Trafficking Helpline –
      +34 900 105 090

    Charities and Support Networks

  5. Hope for Justice (UK and Europe)
    • Website: hopeforjustice.org
    • Provides rescue and support services for trafficking victims.
  6. Caritas Internationalis (Europe-wide)
    • Website: caritas.org
    • Offers social services, legal aid, and reintegration support.
  7. Migrant Help (UK)
    • Website: migranthelpuk.org
    • Assists migrants and victims of exploitation with safe accommodation and advice.

    8. Educational Resources

    • European Crime Prevention Network (EUCPN)
      • Website: eucpn.org
      • Offers reports, campaigns, and research on human trafficking prevention.
    • UNODC Human Trafficking Portal
      • Website: unodc.org
      • Contains global and European data on trafficking trends and legal frameworks.

    By utilising these resources, you can learn more about human trafficking, recognize its signs, and find avenues to report suspicious activities or support victims. Every action helps to combat this pervasive crime.


    Contact us

    Have questions or need support? Do not hesitate to reach out through our Contact Us section. Whether you need help, or are looking for more information, we are here to listen and provide assistance.
